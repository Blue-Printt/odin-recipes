Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a universally beloved, oven-baked flatbread typically consisting of a leavened wheat dough base topped with savory tomato sauce and melted cheese. It can be customized with a variety of meats, vegetables, and herbs, and baked at high temperatures until the crust is golden and crispy.

Ingredients:

  • 3 - 4 cups bread flour or all-purpose flour
  • 1 and a half cup warm water
  • 2 third teaspoons instant or active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: Cornmeal or semolina flour for dusting the pan
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes (or pizza sauce)
  • 2 - 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional seasonings: Salt, dried oregano, and a pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Toppings: Pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, cooked sausage, or fresh basil

Steps to Cook:

  1. Mix the warm water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl, and let it rest for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Stir in the flour, olive oil, and salt until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface (or in a stand mixer) for 5 - 7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise for 60 - 90 minutes (or overnight in the fridge) until it doubles in size.
  5. While the dough rises, prepare your sauce by mixing the crushed tomatoes, olive oil, salt, oregano, and pepper flakes in a bowl.
  6. Chop your desired vegetables, meats, and grate your mozzarella cheese.
  7. Preheat your oven as high as it goes (ideally 450°F to 475°F).
  8. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into 1 or 2 balls.
  9. On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough to about a 10 to 12-inch circle.
  10. Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet or a pizza stone dusted with cornmeal to prevent sticking.
  11. Brush the edges of the dough with a little olive oil. Spread a thin layer of sauce, add cheese, and layer your toppings.
  12. Bake the pizza in your preheated oven for 10 - 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden-brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  13. Let it cool for 2 minutes, slice, and serve.
